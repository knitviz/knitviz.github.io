Blueprinting Information Architecture

my role on this project: information architect, analyst
tools used: pen, paper, visio

Purpose:

This assignment was to develop an evaluative blueprint of selected portions of the Arduino website and to use that blueprint to analyze and critique the information architecture.

The following sections of the arduino.cc site were blueprinted and analyzed:

  • Home Page + Global / Persistent Navigation
  • Buy
  • Download
  • Products + All Content in Section
  • Learning + All Content in Section
  • Forum
  • Support + All Content in Section
  • Blog
  • Footer + Contact Us, About Us, and Careers

Hello and Welcome!

My name is Malena Hansen, and I'm a passionate user researcher who enjoys crafting a well defined information architecture and content strategy.

I've spent the last 2 years learning UX best practices and refining my craft through the Kent State University Master's of Science in Information Architecture and Knowledge Management with a User Experience Design concentration.

Currently presented are my favorite class projects. Take a look around, and if you have questions or comments, I'd love to hear them.

  • Blueprinting Information Architecture - analyzing content and information architecture of a commercial website, with the purpose of making suggestions on how it could be improved
  • Library Redesign - reviewing the existing information architecture and content strategy to determine how best to bring a library's website into the current web development age
  • Lunch Buddy Money mobile application - solving the problem of managing a child's lunch money account, includes link to proto.io prototype

About MK Designs

Hello! It's nice to meet you, my name is Malena Hansen, and I'm excited to share my favorite projects (so far) with you.

I'm current a Master's of Science in Information Architecture and Knowledge Management with a User Experience Design concentration (insert catch-up breath here) student at Kent State University, with an expected May 2018 graduation. It's been a 2-year adventure and I'm ready to take on organizing the world with my passion for information architecture and user research.

Please use the menu above to navigate my favorite projects:

  • Lunch Buddy Money mobile application - solving the problem of managing a child's lunch money account
  • Blueprinting Information Architecture - analyzing content and information architecture of a commercial website, with the purpose of making suggestions on how it could be improved
  • Library Redesign - reviewing the existing information architecture and content strategy to determine how best to bring a library's website into the current web development age
